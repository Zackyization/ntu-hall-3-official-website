FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about Hall 3.

General Inquiries

What is Hall?

Hall is a place of residence on campus for the students of NTU.

How many Halls of Residences are there in NTU?

There are currently 23 halls.

What are the requirements for applying for a Hall?

As a freshman, there are no requirements for staying in a hall. However, most freshmen will be given a random hall based on availability.

As a senior, there are several ways to obtain a guaranteed place in hall. The most common method is to obtain points. Each hall might have different requisites.

How do you apply for a Hall?

Hall applications are open to all.
For freshmen and senior applications alike, click here for further details.

How much does it cost to stay in Hall?

All rental fees can be seen here. Rental fees vary from year to year.

Can I apply with a roommate?

Yes. All you need to do is fill in the particulars of your roommate when applying for a hall. If you do not apply with a roommate, one will be randomly assigned to you.

Can I apply for a single room?

Priority is given to seniors, year 2 and above, for the allocation of single rooms.

Hall 3 is located near the academic centre of NTU, next to NIE. We are also situated in a bustling cluster of halls, including Hall 16, Hall 12, and Hall 13.
