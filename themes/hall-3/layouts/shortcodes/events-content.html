Events

Residing in Hall 3 entails you to a variety of events, ranging from Freshmen Orientation to Halloween, there is no shortage of an eventful university life!

The biggest annual event:

Yellow Tee Day

The biggest Hall 3 event every year! Celebrate the anniversary of Hall 3 by partaking in a lineup of activities.


Usually taking place near the end of every academic year, celebrate everything Hall 3 by taking part in carnival games, and enjoy the free food. You also stand a chance to win lucky draw prizes!

Dinner & Dance

Dinner and Dance, otherwise known as D&D, is an annual event that takes place in Hall 3 after every FOP. Located at different locations every year, a night of festivities will ensure that all hall residents will have a good time. Not only is there food, but there is also entertainment in the form of performances from the hall's own residents, and sometimes even outside entertainment!


Every D&D is also accompanied with a Hall Pageant, whereby at the end of the night, a Hall King and Queen will be crowned.

A night to celebrate our cultural groups, and also a chance for them to show off their skills and hard work! Absolut3, (Dance) Jamband and Vipers (Cheer) are sure to sweep you off your feet and leave you dazzled with their performances! Don't miss out on this incredible night of lights, music, and entertainment!

Joint‐Hall Halloween

Joint Hall Halloween allows you to interact with friends from halls 16,12 and 13! So take the chance to step away from the books and connect with friends through the events planned. It's gonna be a scary night but if you have friends around, there's nothing to be scared of! And of course, don't forget to dress up! Hearsay there are prizes for the best dressed people at the end of the night!

IHG/IHRG

Every year, NTU organizes the Hall Olympiad- which consists of Inter-Hall games (IHG) and Inter-Hall Recreational games (IHRG). Here, residents from all 23 halls come together in the spirit of friendly competition. In addition, the HO provides a platform for residents to showcase their talents and to recognize them for all the hard work put in.


So, what are you waiting for? Take the chance to represent the hall in a sport or recreational games and soar Hall 3's flag high!

Elite Night Cycling

ENC is a annual long distance cycling event held at night! Participants can sign up for a night filled with a lot of cycling and games along the way. Gather your squad and get ready for an exhilarating adventure from late night till the first light of dawn. And not to worry, the event is designed to be novice-friendly, so all participants can enjoy a night of fun under the stars!

HOCC

The Hall Olympiad Closing Ceremony (HOCC) is an annual event held to mark the end of Hall Olympiad for the academic year.


HOCC celebrates the achievements of all our recreational games participants and athletes, aptly closing off with the annual dance competition for all our hall dancers. Our dancers never fail to produce a heartfelt and tear-jerking performance and we are incredibly proud of Absolut3 for their time and effort to make this possible!

Past vs Present

Get geared up to face off with ex-residents alumni of Hall 3 in various sports and recreational games and prove that the present is better than the past! It also allows residents (past or present) to bond over the sharing of pointers and competition experience throughout the day!

Joint‐Hall Iftar Night

The Hall 3 Iftar Night aims to celebrate diversity and inclusivity through various activities. These include a talk by a Muslim representative on the Muslim journey and Ramadan traditions, such as "buka puasa". Additional activities such as breaking fast together and a video presentation promotes cultural understanding and engagement among residents. The event will also feature diverse cultural foods for attendees to enjoy, allowing them to fully immerse themselves!

Yellow Tee Day

Yellow Tee Day, the biggest event of the year, is held to celebrate the hall 3's birthday as well as to get residents together to enjoy a myriad of mass games and activities. Not to mention the various performances put up by the various cultural groups and a lucky draw at the end! Lastly, not forgetting the iconic hanging of the hall 3 shirts over LRA!

Hall Appreciation Day

Hall Appreciation Day (HAD) looks to acknowledge and extend the Hall's appreciation to residents who contributed greatly towards the hall. Residents who are proficient in sports, recreational games and cultural activities are awarded the Colours, Medals and Cultural awards respectively. Outstanding residents who contribute in multiple areas are awarded the Meritorious award.
