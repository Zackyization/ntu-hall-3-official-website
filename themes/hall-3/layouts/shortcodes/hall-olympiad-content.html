Hall Olympiad

The Hall Olympiad (HO) is an annual event in NTU, where residents from all 23 halls come together in the spirit of friendly competition.


The aim of the HO is not only to bond residents within their respective halls, but also across all 23, igniting the NTU spirit and forging strong friendships.
