Power sockets and internet access (via ethernet cable) available.

Crest and Logo

The crest features an armored winged lion, combining the attributes of a lion and an eagle to symbolize the strength, courage, and wisdom of Hall 3's residents.

The lion's design highlights its power, speed, cunning, and wisdom, while the eagle's wings represent courage.

The armor is adorned with three octagons and three vertical stripes in 'black-blue-black,' representing the three blocks of the Hall and its colors, respectively, signifying the armor as a protective shield for the Hall.
